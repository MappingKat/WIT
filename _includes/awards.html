Awards

The WIT Foundation recognises the accomplishments of women in technology, and the organisations that recognise their value, through a variety of awards. Alongside the glory, the award winners can also expect great prizes to be received.

  • WIT Student of Vision Award

    WIT Student of Vision Award recognises a student who has a unique vision of making a difference in media and technology, and has already started making initiatives – large or small – to realise that vision. The Student of Vision is passionate about innovative technologies and inspires others to broaden the participation of women in media and technology.

    Winner of the 2015 WIT Student of Vision Award:

    Malin Arvidsson, Computer Science Student KTH, Communications Manager Flic, President Malvina (KTH Female Network)

  • WIT Leadership Award

    WIT Leadership Award recognises a leading woman who has led an important technology development or innovation, made a significant contribution to the media and technology industries, and inspires others.

    Winner of the 2015 WIT Leadership Award:

    Eva Fors, COO Microsoft Sweden

  • WIT Entrepreneurial Award

    WIT Entrepreneurship Award recognises a female entrepreneur that possesses the drive, managerial skills and leadership traits that inspire others to follow her lead. She has created a successful and viable media or technology venture startup (within a larger organisation or stand-alone), turning it from idea to finished product through assertive risk taking and innovation.

    Winner of the 2015 WIT Entrepreneurial Award:

    Elina Berglund, CTO & co-founder Natural Cycles.

    Nomination for this year will open in February 2016.
