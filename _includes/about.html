About

Diversity is an issue within many facets of life. Women In Technology (WIT) believes that we can do something to change the lack of gender diversity in the media and technology industries. The organization brings together key influencers, community members, entrepeneurs and thought-provokers.

The Mission:

To inspire talented women to consider a future in media and technology by providing the network and experience of successful women and men from the corporate and entrepreneurial worlds.
